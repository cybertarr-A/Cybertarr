# CAS-0001 — Digital Embryo

## Status

Accepted

## Purpose

The Digital Embryo is the smallest computational organism in Cybertarr.

It possesses no knowledge and no reasoning ability.

Its only purpose is to exist, evolve, and eventually learn.

## Initial Capabilities

* Exists
* Ages
* Maintains internal state
* Executes a heartbeat

## Initial Limitations

* No memory
* No language
* No planning
* No reasoning
* No learning
* No goals

## Design Principles

1. Intelligence must emerge.
2. Knowledge cannot be hardcoded.
3. Every capability must be experimentally justified.
4. Observation precedes understanding.

## Success Criteria

The embryo maintains a stable heartbeat and internal state across continuous execution.
